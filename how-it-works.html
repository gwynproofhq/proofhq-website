How it works

Configure. Connect. Apply. Produce proof.

Four steps from playbook configuration to evidence-backed deal reviews and commit validation.

1

Configure the playbook

Encode qualification criteria, stage rules, inspection prompts, coaching standards, and commit expectations inside PROOF.

2

Connect evidence sources

Sync your CRM (Salesforce or HubSpot), connect Google Workspace or Microsoft 365 for email and calendar signals, and capture rep updates via Voice-to-CRM. Evidence flows in automatically.

3

Apply logic in workflow

Run the configured standard across prep, deal reviews, coaching, inspection, and progression.

4

Produce outputs and next actions

Generate review support, commit validation, warnings, and next best proof without rebuilding the deal from scratch.

For direct buyers

Teams use PROOF to reduce prep, run better reviews, standardise inspection, and improve confidence in commits.

For partners

Agencies and methodology firms use the same flow to audit, configure, deploy, and extend their client work.

Simple by design

Configure once. Evidence flows in from CRM, email, calendar, and voice. PROOF does the rest.

The operational path from configuration to deployment is designed to take days, not months. Start with CRM, layer in email and calendar, and run your first evidence-backed deal review within the first week.
