Five evidence systems — designed as outcomes, not feature checklists. Together, they form a continuous evidence system — from selling activity to forecast integrity.

AI, responsibly

AI-assisted evidence extraction — with human confirmation.

PROOF uses AI to extract structured evidence from notes and transcripts — but never silently changes your CRM. AI surfaces what's there. You decide what gets written back.

Transcript ingestion

Paste a call transcript and PROOF extracts evidence-relevant signals — competition, RFP path, stakeholder changes, and process risk.

Evidence suggestions

PROOF surfaces the next evidence required, based on stage and evidence gaps — guiding reps to what matters without admin overhead.

Full auditability

Every AI recommendation is traceable to its source. Overrides are explicit. Nothing is black-box. Every suggestion is cited.
